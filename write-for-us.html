Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum word count?

600 words minimum. 800–1,200 is the sweet spot. Longer is fine if the content justifies it — I will not pad thin articles with filler.

Is the backlink really permanent?

Yes. Posts are published on GitHub Pages and will remain live indefinitely. I do not unpublish paid content unless it violates the guidelines above.

Can I make edits after publication?

Minor corrections (typos, broken links) are free. Substantial rewrites or adding new backlinks after the fact are not included.

What gets rejected?

Articles that are clearly AI-generated with no editorial voice, thin content under 600 words, anything off-topic, and anything that would be embarrassing to associate with an academic's professional site.

Can I submit more than one post?

Yes. Repeat submissions are welcome. Each post is priced and reviewed independently.
